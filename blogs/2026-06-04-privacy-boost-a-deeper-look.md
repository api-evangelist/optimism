Privacy Boost is the first privacy SDK for the OP Stack, built by Sunnyside Labs, enabling confidential token transfers and private smart contract logic with a compliance-compatible architecture. This post provides a technical deep-dive into the ZK+TEE hybrid architecture, explaining why prior approaches hit ceilings and why regulators are now pointing in the same direction as this privacy-preserving design.
