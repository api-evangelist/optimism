Roughly 71% of stablecoin transfer volume is already driven by automated systems, and enterprises are deploying agentic capabilities at scale with AI agents executing payments, rebalancing positions, and settling transactions autonomously. The OP Stack is positioned as a proven system to support agentic commerce, offering stablecoin payment infrastructure with built-in handling for limits, KYC, and risk management.
